ACCOMMODATION

At Luxe Waves Hotel, guests can indulge in a range of luxurious accommodations tailored to meet their needs. No matter the choice, guests are treated to unparalleled luxury and comfort, ensuring an unforgettable stay.

STANDARD ROOM

Standard Rooms offer a spacious bedroom with a king-size bed, en-suite bathroom, balcony with ocean view.

Maximum occupants: 2 adults, 1 child

Amenities:

  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Minibar
  • Flat-screen TV
  • Luxury toiletries

PRICE: Starting from $300 per night

PREMIUM ROOM

Premium Rooms offer a bedroom with a king-size bed, separate living area with a sofa bed, en-suite bathrooom, balcony with partial ocean view.

Maximum occupants: 2 adults, 1 child

Amenities:

  • Complimentary breakfast
  • Access to executive lounge
  • Evening cocktails
  • Premium bath amenities

PRICE: Starting from $400 per night

DELUXE ROOM

Deluxe Rooms offer two bedrooms-one with a king-size bed and another with twin beds, spacious living area, two bathrooms, private terrace with ocean view.

Maximum occupants: 3 adults, 2 children

Amenities:

  • Butler service
  • Personalised concierge
  • Access to exclusive beach area
  • Complimentary airport transfer

PRICE: Starting from $600 per night

EXECUTIVE SUITE

Executive Suites offer two bedrooms-one with a king-size bed and another with twin beds, separate living area, dining area, two bathrooms, private balcony with ocean view.

Maximum occupants: 3 adults, 2 children

Amenities:

  • In-room dining
  • Turndown service
  • Access to fitness center and spa

PRICE: Starting from $800 per night

DIAMOND SUITE

Diamond Suites offer two bedrooms-each with a king-size bed, separate living area with a sofa bed, dining area, three bathrooms, private terrace with an ocean view.

Maximum occupants: 4 adults, 4 children

Amenities:

  • Private check-in/check-out
  • Complimentary airport transfers
  • Priority restaurant reservations

PRICE: Starting from $1,000 per night

PRESIDENTIAL SUITE

Presidential Suites offer three bedrooms-two with king size beds and one with twin beds, spacious living area, dining area, three bathrooms, private rooftop terrace with Jaccuzi and paranomic ocean view.

Maximum occupants: 6 adults, 4 children

Amenities:

  • Personalized villa host
  • Private chef on request
  • Exclusive access to VIP events

PRICE: Starting from $1,500 per night
